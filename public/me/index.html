Dr. Jonathan Kent

I am passionate about drawing insights from data using Bayesian statistics and machine learning, and I enjoy exploring large, complex, and atypical datasets.

Before getting a PhD in quantitative social science, I spent 10 years in Washington, DC and New York City advising political and business leaders on strategic communications. The skills from this stage of my career help me share sophisticated data science in an approachable and effective way, always emphasizing visualization.
